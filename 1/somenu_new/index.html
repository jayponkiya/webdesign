SOMENU APP HAS THE FOLLOWING FEATURES

Be Digital With Extra Ordinary Menu

Our app helps you to create a digital menu for your different food service. One can easily transfer a physical menu to a digital menu using our app and categories your various items as per your choice.

Editable or Changeable

One can easily add, remove or change various items, their prices, their categories by using our app.

Cost-Saver

Using our app you can save menu printing costs and also help you to save your time.

ContactLess / Safe to Use

In this pandemic COVID - 19 situations, the digital menu is safer to use. Without any contact, one can scan the menu & order different foods as per their choice.

Various Themes

We provide various customizable themes that allow you to change or modify your menu colour and style to attract your customers.

Customer Feedback

For better progress customer feedback is important. SOMENU allows users to provide their feedback or rating by using our app. So, you can easily analyze your customer’s reviews & improve them.

Easily Manageable

One can create multiple menus with various price fields. For example, Pizza - Small, medium, & large. You can also create various menus like holiday menu, Sunday menu, etc that can be easily manageable.

Share Your Visual Menu

Mouth to mouth marketing is the best part of any business, and that things can be done by customers. Let your menu go viral using our app. We provide an inbuilt feature to share your visual menu using WhatsApp.

Customer Care Support

We’re a friendly bunch to help you anyways. Feel free to ask any kind of queries regarding our application, our dedicated staff will contact you ASAP.

About Us

Show your customer they are king,
don't make them wait for the menu even for a minute.

SOMENU serves innovative & customized solutions for your menu book presentation just with a single scan. We allow you to create and manage digital menus for your Restaurant, Hotels, Bar, Cafe, Tea-Stall, sweet shop, Ice-cream parlour, etc.

Don't let your customer get confused between the pages of the menu book and scroll pages to and back from soups to main course back to back to remember what they want to order.

No need to carry a menu and letting your customers wait till they order. Put your QR code on the tables and lets customers scan and get the whole menu of your food service.

SOMENU app is developed with the latest and advanced technology. One can easily create a unique digital menu in 3 or 4 steps only for your fine dining, hotel, cafe, bar, etc. SOMENU allows you to add, delete or manage your different food items with various categories to make everything transparent to your customers.
